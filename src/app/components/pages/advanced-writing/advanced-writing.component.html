WRITING SAMPLES  

For Northeastern's Interdisciplinary Advanced Writing class, students are required to complete several writing projects. You may find these writing projects below.

Genre, Rhetoric, and Discourse Analysis

An analysis of “The Racist History Behind Facial Recognition” by New York Times graphics editor Sahil Chinoy

Accessible Front-End Development

A reference document describing common mistakes and solutions when creating accessible websites.

Robot Recruiters: The Rise of AI in Recruiting

A literature review examining the circumstances the led to use of AI in recruiting practices, as well as the potentially consequences of such use.

Reflection

A reflection of the course's work.
